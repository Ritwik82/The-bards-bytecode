The Bard's Bytecode

An LNMIIT web challenge in three acts.

Somewhere between candlelight and command line, a play was written that a compiler can understand. Its characters are variables, its insults are arithmetic, and when the time is right a single line will be spoken that does not belong on any stage.

That line is the only thing that matters. Everything else is theatre.
